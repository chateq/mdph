The goal of the project is to help users complete their application files. The platform will include several guided flows, each made up of a series of questions that users need to answer.
Once the questionnaire is completed, the system should trigger different types of processing based on the user’s responses. In particular, it should be able to generate conditional PDF documents depending on the case and the answers provided. AI must also be integrated, as the platform is expected to deliver personalized responses and tailored feedback.
Some documents will need to be analyzed, including health-related documents, although the exact scope and process are not yet fully defined. Because of the sensitive nature of this data, it would be important to ensure that all information sent to the AI is anonymized beforehand.
The project also includes a matching or referral component. After completing a flow and receiving an initial assessment, users should have the option to pay for access to a professional who can guide them further or connect them with an expert in the relevant field.
For payments, the client does not want to use Stripe and would prefer to use Mollie instead.
In summary, the idea is as follows: users go through a guided flow, answer a series of questions, and once their responses are submitted, a processing workflow is launched. This workflow includes AI-based analysis, personalized feedback, and the generation of relevant documents. After receiving this first level of feedback, users can then choose to be supported by a professional.
